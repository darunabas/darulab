The Daru Lab is moving to Stanford University Jan 2023!

We are thrilled to announce that the Daru Lab will be moving to the superb Department of Biology at Stanford University in January 2023! We will continue our work in biogeography and ecology of plants, but we are also excited about new research directions and collaborations!

  • We will be part of Stanford's Department of Biology
  • Our lab will be located in the Gilbert Biological Sciences Building
  • We're hiring at all levels! If you are interested in PhD opportunities in the Daru lab, please fill out this form.
